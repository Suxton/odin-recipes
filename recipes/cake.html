One Bowl Chocolate Cake III

Description

This is a rich and moist chocolate cake. It only takes a few minutes to prepare the batter. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup and 3 quarters of all-purpose flour
  • 3 quarters of a cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • half a cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
